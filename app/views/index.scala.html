What is Open Power Quality?

Open Power Quality (OPQ) is a project developed at the University of Hawaii at Manoa which allows for distributed power quality analysis.

Find Out More

Community Data Sharing Initiative

OPQ device users may choose to share power quality information with the public. That information is publicly available in one of two formats. A map view for visual analysis, and the ability to download a comma separated value file of power quality information.
